No data is stored or transmitted externally. All app functionalities are performed locally on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

What's My GPA does not use or integrate with any third-party services or analytics providers.

4. Children’s Privacy

As no data is collected, the app is safe for use by individuals of all ages, including children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
